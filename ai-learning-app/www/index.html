Mathematical Concept

SVM constructs a dividing hyperplane that maximizes the "margin" between classes. The points touching the margins are the Support Vectors (highlighted with glowing rings). A low C penalty allows some margins violations (wider boundary), while high C forces strict classification.

Ensemble Concept

A single Decision Tree splits data orthogonally, leading to blocky boundaries. A Random Forest trains multiple independent trees on bootstrapped (sampled) data. By averaging the probability outputs of all trees, the ensemble generates a smoother, generalized boundary that reduces overfitting.
